Recreation of Bob & Betty Beyster Building in Virtual Reality

Plant Augmented Reality Game

In a pair, I created an Augmented Reality Game in the Unity Game Engine where players can plant trees to earn coins. Occasionally, a squirrel will spawn and reduce the amount of money that a tree can produce. Oh no! We destroy the squirrel by throwing acorns at it. In rare occasions, a big boss (shiba dog) will appear and they need 3 acorns to destroy! It is a very fun game.

  • Learn more

Final Project

In a group of five, we created a final project for EECS 498: Extended Reality & Society class that covered VR technologies. We built a realistic fire simulation titled Inferno that helped players figure out what to do during the event of a fire. This was built using Unreal Engine 5 and focused on teaching players fire preparedness tactics in a fun and interactive manner.
