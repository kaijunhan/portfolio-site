A2GO Squirrel Augmented Reality Gamey

The video demonstrates the features that we have implemented in the Squirrel Go Game for our virtual environment, including a map implemented with Mapbox, with squirrel gameobjects that destroy upon collision with an acorn, we managed to create trees which appear in both interaction and exploration mode, these trees grow every frame and update currency, custom character movement, custom seeds, multiple landmarks which unlocked seeds, image recognition with ARtoolkit and both development and production modes.

Our custom feature was a shiba dog that required more acorns to destroy and barked upon collision with our acorn.
