Recreation of the BBB in Virtual Reality

\

The video demonstrates the features that we have implemented in the Bob & Betty Beyster Building for our virtual environment, including 3d assets which model the BBB space, with interactions built from the base affordance class, we managed to create light switch functionality, exiting the application through the door, fire alarm which toggles lights, passerby movement, NPCs with dialogue options, whiteboard drawing functionality and a custom game.

Our custom game enables the player to play a simple VR first person shooter where a gun is spawned where we are able to shoot enemies aka "ghosts" which pass through the wall into the Bob & Betty Beyster building before they reach the orb in the center of the room. The game is a thrilling first person shooter that involves quick reflexes and good aim.
